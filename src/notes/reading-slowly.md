I read faster than I should. I skim when I should sit. There's a difference between having read something and actually absorbing it — I often end up with the former thinking I got the latter. Slowing down feels inefficient until you realize that reading something twice because you rushed the first time is the actual inefficiency. Taking a note while reading, even a bad one, forces enough pause to turn skimming into something that sticks.
