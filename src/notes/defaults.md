Most decisions are defaults. You don't choose your morning routine every morning — you just do it. You don't re-evaluate your editor config each time you open a file. Defaults compound: good ones free up attention for things that actually deserve it, bad ones quietly drain it. It's worth occasionally auditing the defaults you didn't consciously choose, because at some point someone else chose them for you, and that person may not have had your interests in mind.
