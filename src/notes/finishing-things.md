There's a specific satisfaction in finishing a small thing completely — not mostly done, not "good enough for now," but actually closed. It's different from finishing a large thing. Large projects end with relief. Small things end with a clean click, like a well-fitted drawer. I try to notice that feeling when it happens, because it's easy to undervalue the small completions while chasing the large ones.
