A good variable name is a tiny act of documentation. `data` tells you nothing. `filteredUsersByRegion` tells you exactly what you're holding and where it came from. The few extra seconds it takes to name something well pays back every time someone — including future you — reads that line. Bad names don't just slow comprehension, they actively mislead. Renaming things is one of the highest-leverage refactors you can do on an existing codebase.
